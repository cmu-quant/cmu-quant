Quant Club @
Carnegie Mellon
The CMU Quant Club focuses on the study of quantitative finance, a branch of finance that uses advanced mathematical and computational techniques. We host events that help undergraduates learn about and connect with the quantitative finance industry; these include mock interviews, resume workshops, lecture series, seminars, and our flagship events, the Quantathon and the Market Making Game.

Flagship Events
