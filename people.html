CMU Quant Club 2021-2022 Board Members

Gan Yang

President

Gan Yang is a junior from MCS majoring in Computational Finance. Since young, he has always found himself drawn to math, puzzles and competition, things he knew he would find in the quantitative finance industry. Thus, on top of pursuing his interests in pure mathematics, he has been actively exploring the field, such as through his recent Quantitative Research Internship at Worldquant Singapore. As President, he hopes to bolster the quant community at CMU as much as those before have inspired him. In his free time, Gan enjoys drinking unhealthy amounts of coffee and listening to jazz.

Cynthia Dong

Vice President of Administration

Cynthia is a junior at Tepper pursuing a degree in Computational Finance. She is particularly interested in trading and has spent the past year doing mathematical finance research. Besides her dedication to finance, she loves baking, oil painting, and swimming. She is excited to work with everyone this year in Quant Club!

Benjamin Mok

Vice President of Finance

Benjamin is a junior studying Computational Finance and planning to minor in Machine Learning. He is passionate about applying quantitative skills to solve problems and thus was drawn to the quant finance industry. Benjamin is particularly interested in machine learning, finance and their intersections. He has done finance research freshman summer and a data analysis/ML internship at Alliance Healthcare in Singapore during his sophomore summer. In his free time he enjoys skiing, playing tennis and travelling with friends.

Jingchun Quan

Vice President of Projects

Jingchun is a junior pursuing a dual degree in Computational Finance and Economics & Statistics. She is interested in trading and markets, specifically the intersection between quantitative frameworks and the intuition required for the ever-changing markets. Jingchun enjoys art and painting, as well as baking desserts like cakes and macarons. She is excited to work with the other officers to bring new opportunities and events to Quant Club and the CMU campus.

Josie Li

Vice President of Public Relations

Josie Li is a junior majoring in Computational Finance. She is interested in using knowledge from math, statistics, and computer science to solve real-world problems in the financial industry. In her free time, she likes cooking, watching films, and going to concerts.

Karl Xiao

He is also passionate about building better tools for traders. Karl wants to finish exploring every nook and cranny of Pittsburgh by the time he graduates, and when stuck at home he's probably on the hunt for good old movies.
